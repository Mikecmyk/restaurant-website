About Us

Our History

Mboga Bistro was founded in 2020 with a mission to bring healthy and delicious meals inspired by Kenyan tradition and international flavors.

Our Mission

To serve quality meals with love, affordability, and a touch of home. We believe in fresh ingredients, great service, and happy customers.

Our team is made up of passionate chefs, friendly waiters, and a management team that loves what they do. Everyone here plays a key role in making your dining experience special.
